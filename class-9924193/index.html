How to Catch Ws in Kart Bros

👀 Hold Your Items

If you are in first place, don't just waste your shield or speed boost. Hold onto it until someone shoots a missile at you.

💨 Drift Every Corner

If you just turn normally, you lose massive speed. Always tap shift to drift into corners so you exit them flying.

🚀 Cut the Grass

If you have a mushroom or speed boost, you can drive straight over the grass and dirt to skip entire corners of the track.

🎯 Look Backwards

Keep an eye on the mini-map to see if your friends are creeping up behind you so you can drop a trap right in their face.
