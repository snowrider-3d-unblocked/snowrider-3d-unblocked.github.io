How to Catch Ws in Dreadhead Parkour

👀 Don't Stop Running

The physics engine rewards momentum. If you brake before a jump, you won't make the gap. Trust your speed.

🤸 Flip for Cash

If you have major airtime, hold the left or right arrow to flip. It drops extra coins that you need to unlock sick skins.

🤕 Protect Your Head

If you land a flip upside down, your neck snaps and you restart at the checkpoint. Always land on your feet.

⏳ Slide Early

The slide animation takes a millisecond to trigger. If a saw blade is coming, hit 'S' slightly earlier than you think.
