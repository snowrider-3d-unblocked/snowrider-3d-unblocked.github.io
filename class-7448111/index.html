How to Catch Ws in Temple Run 2

👀 Look at the Distance

As the game gets faster, looking right at your character will get you killed. Look at the top edge of the screen so you can react to turns early.

💰 Upgrade the Coin Magnet

Spend your first few batches of gold upgrading the magnet. It allows you to focus purely on dodging without risking your life for coins.

🛡️ Pop the Shield Early

When your power meter fills up, don't save it. Pop the shield immediately. It gives you one free mistake, which is clutch at high speeds.

🧘 Stay Centered

Unless you are dodging a broken path or grabbing a specific line of coins, always try to run down the exact middle of the track.

FAQ

Can my school laptop run Temple Run 2 Unblocked?

Yes! The WebGL graphics are optimized to run smoothly on Chromebooks without causing massive lag spikes.

Do I lose my upgrades when I die?

Nope, you keep all the coins you collected. Go to the menu to buy permanent upgrades for your power-ups.

Can I unlock different characters?

Yep. Once you stack enough cash, you can buy different explorers, each with unique default power-ups.

How do you do the minecart parts?

Just use the left and right arrows to tilt the cart so you don't fall off the edge of the broken tracks.
