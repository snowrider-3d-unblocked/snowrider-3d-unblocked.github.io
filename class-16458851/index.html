How to Catch Ws in Mad Day

👀 Focus on the Truck

Your truck is your best defense. Upgrade its armor and mounted guns first, so you can travel further before having to run on foot.

🚀 Spam the Jump

When driving, your truck actually has boosters. Hitting jump at the right time helps you dodge massive alien lasers and pits.

💰 Grab the Magnets

If you see a magnet power-up, risk it all to grab it. It sucks in all the coins so you can buy upgrades way faster.

🎩 Buy the Hats

They look goofy, but the hats in the shop actually give you passive stat boosts like extra health and better coin drops.

FAQ

Can I play Mad Day Unblocked at school?

Yes! It’s a very safe, cartoony action game that easily gets past school network blockers.

Will I lose my truck if the bell rings?

Nope. The game uses local storage to save your cash and upgrades. Just don't clear your history.

Why does the game keep changing to a platformer?

That's the core mechanic! When your truck takes too much damage, it explodes, and you have to finish the run on foot.

Is there an end to the game?

Yep. If you upgrade enough, you will eventually reach the massive alien boss and rescue your octopus.

Blast the Aliens in Mad Day Unblocked

The plot of this game is absolutely wild: aliens invade Earth and steal your pet octopus, so you load up a massive truck with rockets and go to war. Mad Day unblocked perfectly blends driving and run-and-gun platforming. You start the level driving a heavily armed vehicle, running over aliens and blasting obstacles. Massive Garage Upgrades
