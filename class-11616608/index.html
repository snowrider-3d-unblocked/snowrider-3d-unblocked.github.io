How to Catch Ws in Bob The Robber 4

👀 Watch the Camera Timing

Security cameras pan back and forth. Hide in the shadows, wait for them to look away, and then sprint past.

💰 Loot Everything

Don't just go for the main objective. Search every cabinet and box for extra cash to buy better gear between levels.

🏃 Patience is Key

If you rush, you will trigger the alarm. Once the alarm goes off, a massive detective will hunt you down. Take your time.

🔌 Cut the Power

Look for electrical boxes to disable lasers and cameras temporarily. It's the biggest brain move.

FAQ

Can I play Bob The Robber 4 Unblocked at school?

Yes, it’s a browser-based puzzle game that sneaks past school firewalls just as well as Bob sneaks past guards.

What happens if I get caught?

If you step in a camera's green light or a guard spots you, the alarm triggers and you have to restart the level. Major L.

Do my gadgets save?

Yep, your money and gadget inventory save locally, so you can keep upgrading your thief loadout.

Is this game actually hard?

The first few levels are a breeze, but later stages require actual strategy and perfect timing.
