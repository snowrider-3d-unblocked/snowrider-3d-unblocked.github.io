The Rules of Merging

It’s super simple to learn, incredibly hard to master:

  • Swiping: Every time you swipe, ALL tiles move in that direction.
  • Merging: Two tiles with the same number crash into each other and combine (4+4=8).
  • New Tiles: A new '2' or '4' spawns after every move. Don't let the board fill up.

How to Catch Ws in 2048

👀 Pick a Corner

The absolute biggest strategy is to keep your highest number tile stuck in one corner (like the bottom right) and never swipe away from it.

🧱 Build a Wall

Keep your bottom row completely filled with big numbers so your highest tile doesn't accidentally move when you swipe left or right.

🐢 Don't Rush

Spamming the arrow keys might get you to 128 fast, but it will ruin your board layout. Think 2 moves ahead.

🚫 Never Swipe Up

If your big tile is in the bottom corner, swiping up pulls it out of safety and ruins the whole run. Just don't do it.
