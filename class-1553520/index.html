How to Catch Ws in Stick Merge

👀 Don't Fill Your Board

If you buy too many low-level guns and fill up your inventory grid, you won't have space to merge them. Keep it organized.

🎯 Aim for the Head

Even though your stickman shoots automatically, you still control the crosshair. Headshots kill way faster and save you from taking damage.

📺 Shoot the Supply Drops

Sometimes crates fall from the sky during combat. Shoot them! They drop massive amounts of cash and high-level free weapons.

🔫 Keep Upgrading

Enemies get super tanky later on. If you aren't constantly merging to create sniper rifles and lasers, you will get overrun.

FAQ

Can I play Stick Merge Unblocked at school?

Yes! Because the game is mostly managing a grid of items, it flies completely under the radar of school firewalls.

Do I lose my guns if I die in combat?

Nope. You just fail the level and go back to your armory. Your guns are safe.

Is there an end to the game?

It's an endless progression game. You just keep merging to see what insane sci-fi weapon you can unlock next.

Why am I doing zero damage?

Your gun level is too low for the wave you are on. Go back to the armory and spend all your cash on merging.
