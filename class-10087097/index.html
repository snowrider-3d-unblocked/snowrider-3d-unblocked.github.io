How to Catch Ws in Jelly Truck

👀 Take It Slow

There is no time limit. Rushing usually causes the truck to bounce wildly and flip over. Just chill and crawl over the obstacles.

🍮 Use the Squish

If a tunnel looks too small, just ram into it. The truck will literally deform and squeeze through if you keep holding the gas.

⚖️ Lean Mid-Air

Whenever you hit a jump, instantly use the left and right arrows to make sure your wheels are facing the ground when you land.

🔄 Wiggle Out of Stuck Spots

If you get jammed in a pit, spam left and right while reversing to wiggle the jelly physics loose.

FAQ

Can I play Jelly Truck Unblocked without Flash?

Yes! Our site uses an HTML5 emulator that brings the classic Flash game back to life without needing any sketchy plugins.

Why does the truck move like that?

The game uses soft-body physics. Basically, the truck is made of Jell-O instead of metal, which makes it super funny to drive.

How do you beat the elevator levels?

You have to drive onto the platform and wait for it to move. Don't hit the gas while it's moving or you'll slide right off.

Is the game hard?

Not really. It's more of a puzzle and patience game than a sweaty reaction tester.
