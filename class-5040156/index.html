How to Catch Ws in Tictactoe

👀 Take the Center Early

The middle square is the most OP spot on the board. If you go first, claim it immediately to control the diagonals.

📐 The Corner Trap

If you control the center, try to grab two opposite corners. It usually forces your opponent into making a mistake.

🐢 Force the Draw

If you mess up your opening, play strictly defensively. A draw is always better than catching a fat L.

🤖 Watch the AI's Patterns

The hard AI won't fall for basic traps. You have to force it into a corner by creating double-threat situations.
