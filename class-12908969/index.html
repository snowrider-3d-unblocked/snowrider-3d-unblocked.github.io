How to Catch Ws in Bad Ice Cream 2

👀 Trap the Monsters

Don't just run away. If a monster is chasing you down an alley, turn around and shoot an ice wall right in its face to trap it.

🏃 Keep Moving

Some enemies can break ice blocks too. Never assume you are 100% safe behind a wall. Always have an escape route.

🍉 Watch the Fruit Spawns

Sometimes new fruit spawns after you collect the first batch. Be ready to change your route immediately.

🤝 Work Together (Co-op)

If playing with a friend, communicate! Have one person act as bait and build walls while the other grabs the fruit.
