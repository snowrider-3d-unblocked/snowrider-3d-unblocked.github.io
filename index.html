How to Catch Ws in Snow Rider 3D Unblocked

🛑 Greed Kills

We all want the presents, but dying for one is stupid. If a gift is sandwiched tightly between two pine trees, just leave it. Surviving is way more important than grabbing one extra box.

🎯 Stay in the Middle

Do not hug the edges of the track. If you are riding the far left or right side, a tree will eventually spawn right in front of you, giving you zero seconds to react.

⏱️ Time Your Jumps

Jumping too early is the easiest way to ruin a good run. Wait until the very last second to hit the spacebar so you actually clear the obstacles instead of landing on them.

👀 Look Ahead

Stop staring directly at your sled. Keep your eyes focused on the top half of the screen so you can spot incoming hazards and plan your path early.
