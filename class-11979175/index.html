Master the Block

It sounds easy until you try it:

  • The Goal: Drop the 1x2 metal block directly down the square hole.
  • Movement: The block flips end-over-end. You have to calculate the geometry so it lines up perfectly.
  • Orange Tiles: Fragile floors! If your block stands vertically on an orange tile, you fall through.
  • Switches: 'O' switches work with any touch. 'X' switches only activate if you stand vertically on them.

How to Catch Ws in Bloxorz

👀 Work Backwards

Look at the hole first. Figure out what position the block needs to be in right next to the hole, and then reverse your steps to the start.

📝 Write Down the Codes

After you beat a level, a 6-digit passcode flashes on the screen. Write it down in your notes! You can type it in later to skip right to that level.

🌉 Watch the Bridges

Hitting a switch might create a bridge, but hitting it again might delete it. Don't trap yourself on an island.

🧠 Take Your Time

There is no time limit. If you rush and spam the arrow keys, you will roll right off the edge and catch an L.

FAQ

Can I play Bloxorz Unblocked without Flash?

Yes! The game has been completely ported to HTML5, so it runs perfectly smooth on school Wi-Fi and Chromebooks.

Why did I fall through the orange tiles?

Orange tiles can't support the weight of the block standing straight up. You have to roll over them laying flat.

What happens when the block splits into two?

Some teleporters split your block into two smaller cubes. You move them independently with the spacebar and have to bring them back together.

How many levels are there?

There are 33 incredibly sweaty levels. Beating all of them without looking up a tutorial makes you officially goated.

Flex Your Big Brain in Bloxorz Unblocked
