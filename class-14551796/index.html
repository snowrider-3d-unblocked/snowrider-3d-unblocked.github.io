The Training Grind

You can't just win races instantly. You have to put in the work:

  • Running: Dodge falling objects to get faster on land.
  • Flying: Click to drag your duck up and down to avoid obstacles.
  • Swimming: Jump over rocks and dive under ships to boost aquatic speed.
  • Energy: Feed your duck skill seed so it doesn't pass out mid-race.

How to Catch Ws in Duck Life

👀 Train Energy First

It doesn't matter how fast your duck is if it runs out of stamina. Buy lots of skill seeds early on to level up its max energy.

💰 Grind for Coins

Use the mini-games to stack coins. You need them for seeds and to buy the sickest hats to flex your dominance.

🦆 Balance Your Stats

Don't just max out running. The tournaments test all three skills, so a weak swimmer will lose the whole race for you.

🏆 Replay the Easy Races

If you're stuck on a hard tournament, go back and replay easier races to farm free coins for more food.
